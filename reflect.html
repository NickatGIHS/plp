This is the reflection on how well I did in my goals over the past semester.

Introduction

I feel that I have progressed a lot with my PLP, especially in the Information & Communications Technology Capability as that was a part of most of my goals.

Change Maker (Podcast Club)

After a semester of attempting to reach your aspiration, how far did you get?

The most I was able to do in the timeframe set for the folio was create the trailer episode and have everything set up for the club. I was not able to host the first meeting until Term 3. If this seems like not much, that is not the case because the setting up of the club involved:

  • Many emails to Student Life
  • Conferring with the Glenunga Dispatch (in person/via email) and Radio Glenunga (via Discord)
  • Deciding on a podcast host and setting up an account
  • Creating the trailer
  • Finding a supervisor for the club
  • Deciding on a room to host the club in

What were the main challenges faced along the way?

The most obvious challenge in the creation of Podcast Club was that I was not able to be a subset of either the Glenunga Dispatch or Radio Glenunga. Me attempting to join the Glenunga Dispatch took 27 days alone.

Another major challenge was that I have never set up a club or a podcast before, and I am not much of a people person (hence why this goal was initially in Risk Taker), so I had to overcome that as well.

How did you progress in improving your chosen Capability/s?

Information and Communications Technology

This is probably the least developed capability of this goal, as I did not have to do much in the way of much computer-related work, surprisingly.

Most of it was handled by either Student Life or transistor.fm (the podcast host).

Critical and Creative Thinking

This goal was not developed much, but it was used a lot in creating and editing the trailer episode, and as I have never edited or recorded a podcast before, this was very new.

Personal and Social

This is almost certainly the Capability that I developed the most.

That is because I was really out of my comfort zone in creating a club (even more so than Risk Taker, arguably) and I am, as mentioned above, not a people person. I don't think I would have started Podcast Club if not for this PLP Goal.
