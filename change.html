Change Maker Goal

For my Change Maker goal, I have decided to start a club at GIHS - Podcast Club. This was originally my Risk Taker goal.

PLAN

I have wanted to start a club since two years ago, and I have wanted to host a podcast-making club in particular since Februaryish last year.

My plan from the start was to go with a podcast host (decided to go with Transistor initially, but wasn't sure for a while and may eventally change to a self-run RSS feed) in making podcast club for Glenunga.

I didn't really have a plan, except for going to Student Life and seeing how it went from there.
However, I did eventually end up with three plans, of which only the last one worked:

  1. Working with the Glenunga Dispatch.
  2. Working with Radio Glenunga.
  3. Working as a standalone club.

The reason these groups to work with were chosen were because in order,

  1. They are a club about news (specifically GIHS's newspaper); that is what I want the podcast to be about.
  2. They are a club about radio, makes sense to have a podcast section.
  3. This was a fallback option if the other two didn't work out; they didn't.

I still do not know why I wasn't able to work with the Glenunga Dispatch, but I wasn't able to work with Radio Glenunga because when I asked to possibly start a podcast, their club was... dead-ish.

Goal

Criteria: Hold first Club Meeting, have at least 1 episode published.

Completed: First episode published Fri 22/05, First meeting not held by PLP folio due date, but held on 31/07.

The SACE capabilities that I am going to develop alongside this goal are:

  • Information and Communications Technology
  • Critical and Creative Thinking
  • Personal and Social

Information and Communications Technology

The opportunities that I will have to develop this capability is:

  • The fact that I have to put the podcast online so that people can listen to it
    • I will compare different plans for podcast hosting, and add tags and so on to podcast episodes when uploading them. Both of these I have not done before.
    • I will also need to learn about what microphones to use, and so on.
    • I also will have the opportunity to find out how to create and edit a podcast.

Critical and Creative Thinking

The opportunities that I will have to develop this capability is:

  • Writing the script
    • I don't want the podcast being too bland, so I need Creativity to come up with new episode & script ideas
    • I will need Critical Thinking to make sure my script makes sense and is relevant and engaging for students at my school.
  • I will need Critical and Creative thinking when facing any challenges that I come across during this project, and to develop my solutions.

Personal and Social

The opportunities that I will have to develop this capability are:

  • The fact that I am creating a club
    • This means that I will not only have to communicate with potential club members, but also with other people (e.g. Mr Mewett) who can assist in setting up and running the club.
    • I will need to be careful in how I choose to communicate information to these people, including being mindful of being as articulate as I can, and communication with them in a timely manner.
    • To get this project off the ground I will need to be mindful of working to the given timeframe.
  • The fact that it is a podcast and so I may need to conduct interviews as part of it.
    • This will involve me interacting with the person being interviewed in a way that will get a successful interview as a result.
